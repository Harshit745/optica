EVENTS

From workshops to seminars and chapter showcases, BVP Optica organizes events that help students explore optics and photonics through hands-on learning, collaboration, and technical discussion.

2024-2025

Quantum Quotient

Quantum Quotient brought together curiosity, problem solving, and technical thinking in a focused chapter event built around emerging concepts and analytical discussion. The event encouraged students to engage with advanced ideas in a collaborative and exploratory format.

AI Solutions

AI Solutions was organized to expose students to practical ways in which intelligent systems can be applied to technical and societal challenges. The event created space for idea exchange, structured problem solving, and discussion around innovation-led thinking.

Case-O-Craze

Case-O-Craze challenged participants to think critically, work through structured scenarios, and present reasoned solutions in a competitive setting. The event emphasized teamwork, analytical ability, and confidence in approaching complex case-based problems.

Aries

Aries was conducted as an engaging chapter activity that blended participation, learning, and student interaction. It gave members a platform to collaborate, contribute ideas, and strengthen their involvement with the larger culture of technical events in the chapter.

IIIT Visit / Collaboration Event

This event highlighted academic engagement and collaborative exposure through interaction connected with IIIT. It provided students with a broader perspective on technical ecosystems, peer learning, and the value of institutional interaction beyond the classroom.
