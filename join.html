Become a Member

Join BVP Optica to become part of a growing student network focused on innovation in optics and photonics. Members gain technical exposure, chapter participation opportunities, and access to a wider academic community.

Join Optica
  • Access to exclusive workshops and seminars
  • Networking with the global optics community
  • Participation in chapter projects and events
  • Exposure to research and innovation opportunities
  • Access to Optica publications and member benefits

Who Can Join?

Students interested in optics, photonics, electronics, communication, research, design, technical operations, and chapter management are encouraged to participate.
