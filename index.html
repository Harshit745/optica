BVP Optica

Optics & Photonics Student Chapter

BVP Optica is the official student chapter at Bharati Vidyapeeth's College of Engineering, New Delhi dedicated to promoting optics and photonics through technical exposure, collaborative learning, and research-driven activities.

The chapter builds an environment where students can explore modern optical science, strengthen practical understanding, and connect with a wider innovation community.

About Optica

Igniting passion for optics and photonics through education, innovation, and collaboration.

Founded 2019
Founder Dr. Yugnanda Puri
